PIAIC

PRESIDENTIAL INITIATIVE FOR ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE AND COMPUTING

Presidential Initiative for Artificial Intelligence and Computing (PIAIC) is a privately funded, not-for-profit educational initiative started by the President of Pakistan, Dr. Arif Alvi, Kazi Rahat Ali, and Zia Khan to help promote education, research, and business opportunities in emerging tech fields like:

  • Cloud Native Computing
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Blockchain
  • Internet Of Things

Cloud Native Computing:

The Cloud Native Computing program prepares students to program in open-source technologies like Linux, Docker, Kubernetes, JavaScript, Node.js, React, and React Native while introducing the development of Microservices and software techniques on cloud platforms like AWS.

Artificial Intelligence:

The artificial intelligence program of PIAIC teaches the foundation of AI technology, programming in languages like Python, Numpy, Pandas, and Tensorflow 2.0. It also includes deploying practical AI solutions and real-life examples of modern AI.

Blockchain:

This program emphasizes understanding blockchains like Ethereum and Hyperledger (Fabric), programming in languages like Solidity and JavaScript, and developing Distributed Apps (DApps) and offering Initial Coin Offerings (ICOs).

Internet of Things:

The Internet of things program introduces open-source technologies like Rust, Embedded Rust, WebAssembly, and Diesel. It also covers developing Micro Services for IoT Edge, IoT Cloud, and voice computing for device control.
